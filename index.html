Projects

Irish Mortgage Calculator

This is a simple mortgage calculator for Ireland. It allows users to calculate their monthly mortgage payments based on the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term.

Tech Stack

It is built using React, TypeScript, and Fluent UI v9, and is hosted on Azure Static Web Apps.

Motivation

When my partner and I were looking to buy our first home, we found it difficult to find a mortgage calculator that let us compare the mortgage options across banks and for each house we were looking at. The available calculators often were more for finding a mortgage product rather than quickly comparing monthly payments for different scenarios.

I made this as a "bring your own terms" mortgage calculator to help us quickly calculate and compare monthly payments for the mortgage options we were already approved for.

Try It Out

You can try out the calculator below or visit the live site at onlinemortgagecalculator.ie.

Goals Bingo

This is a simple bingo game for New Year's Resolutions. It allows users to create a bingo card with their goals and share it with friends.

Tech Stack

It is built using React and TypeScript, and is hosted on Vercel.

It can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on both desktop and mobile devices.

Motivation

I originally built Goals Bingo for myself and my friends as a different take on New Year's resolutions. Instead of a simple pass/fail list written in one evening, we create a bingo card filled with goals of varying difficulty. Over the year we try to complete a row, column, or diagonal, reflecting the idea that life isn't about hitting every goal perfectly, but about continually improving and celebrating the progress you do make.

I made this app so it can be used offline as a PWA, so my friends who were doing a lot of travel could still plan their goals on a plane (and because I just wanted to learn how to make a PWA!).
